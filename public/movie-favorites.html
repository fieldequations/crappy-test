The Best Movies According To Me

My top three movies as of the moment.

The Apartment

There's nothing wasted in this movie. Even the leftover bottle of champagne from the beginning of the movie ties in to the ending.

The Life of Brian

Technically just a pastiche of sketches written by six different writers, but the whole is so much greater than the sum of its parts. Best grammar scene of all time.

Notorious

The emotional struggle between the two protagonists weaves through the real physical danger the heroine faces. And black and white cinematography never looked so gorgeous.
